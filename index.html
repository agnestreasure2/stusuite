STUSUITE

This App consists of three projects which helps to ease users stress.
They are Expense Calculator, Todo_List and an E-book.
The expense calculator helps users to calculate their total budget,
the amount spent and the balance remaining,
while the todo_list helps users to keep track of their schedule
and the E-book helps users to find book of their choice in any category.
